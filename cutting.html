Laser Cutting

Advanced Laser Cutting

Our fiber laser cutting technology offers unmatched precision for intricate designs and complex patterns. With cutting-edge equipment from industry leaders like TRUMPF and Bystronic, we deliver exceptional edge quality and tight tolerances.

  • Precision up to ±0.1mm
  • Clean, burr-free edges
  • Minimal heat-affected zone
  • Ideal for complex designs
Materials: Mild Steel (up to 25mm), Stainless Steel (up to 20mm), Aluminum (up to 15mm), Brass & Copper (up to 10mm)

Water Jet Cutting

High-Pressure Water Jet Cutting

Our water jet cutting technology uses ultra-high pressure water mixed with abrasive particles to cut through virtually any material with exceptional precision. This cold-cutting process eliminates heat-affected zones, making it ideal for heat-sensitive materials.

  • No thermal distortion
  • Cuts virtually any material
  • Environmentally friendly process
  • Perfect for thick materials
Materials: All metals (up to 200mm), Stone, Glass, Composites, Ceramics, Rubber, Plastics

Our High-End Cutting Machinery

We are equipped with the world's most advanced cutting technologies from leading global manufacturers, ensuring unmatched precision, efficiency, and quality for all your metal fabrication needs.

TRUMPF TruLaser 5030 fiber

10kW TruDisk Laser 3000mm × 1500mm Up to 140m/min Made in Germany

The TRUMPF TruLaser 5030 fiber represents the pinnacle of German engineering excellence in laser cutting technology. Manufactured in Ditzingen, Germany, this premium machine features a powerful 10kW TruDisk fiber laser source that delivers exceptional cutting speeds and precision. Operating at wavelengths of 1030-1070nm, this machine can process mild steel up to 25mm, stainless steel up to 20mm, and aluminum up to 15mm with remarkable accuracy of ±0.05mm. Its advanced BrightLine fiber technology ensures clean edges and minimal heat-affected zones, while the intelligent PierceLine function reduces piercing times by up to 80%. The machine's CoolLine feature allows for cutting thick mild steel with minimal heat distortion, making it ideal for intricate designs and high-precision components.

Flow Flying Bridge Waterjet Cutting machine

94,000 PSI 4000mm × 2000mm Pure / Abrasive waterjet Made in USA

The Flow Flying Bridge Waterjet Cutting Machine is a high-performance solution for cold cutting applications requiring precision and material versatility. Engineered in the USA, this advanced machine utilizes ultra-high-pressure waterjet technology capable of delivering pressures up to 94,000 psi, ensuring clean and precise cuts without introducing heat-affected zones. With a cutting bed size of 4000mm × 2000mm, it accommodates large sheets with ease and maintains a repeatable accuracy of ±0.05mm. The Flying Bridge design allows for smooth motion control and excellent structural stability, even at high speeds. Equipped with Flow’s Dynamic Waterjet® and Active Tolerance Control, the system actively compensates for stream lag and taper, ensuring highly accurate cuts on complex geometries. This makes it ideal for cutting metals, stone, glass, composites, and sensitive materials that would otherwise deform under thermal stress.

Durma Sheering Machine

Hydraulic guillotine shear Up to 3100mm 8–15 strokes/min Made in Turkey

The Durma Shearing Machine is a robust and precision-engineered solution designed for high-efficiency sheet metal cutting. Manufactured in Turkey, this hydraulic guillotine shear offers reliable performance for cutting mild steel, stainless steel, and aluminum sheets with clean, burr-free edges. Featuring a cutting length of up to 3100mm and a thickness capacity of up to 12mm, it combines speed and precision through a rigid frame structure and advanced back gauge system. The integrated CNC controller ensures consistent accuracy and allows for programmable cut settings, optimizing workflow and reducing operator fatigue. Equipped with adjustable blade clearance, hydraulic hold-downs, and stroke adjustment.
