Durma Ironwork

3000 mm Up to 20 mm/sec Made in Turkey

The Durma Ironwork Machine is a heavy-duty industrial system engineered for precision metal fabrication and complex profile work. Manufactured in Turkey by Durma, this machine handles steel plates up to 40mm thickness with exceptional accuracy. Featuring a maximum processing width of 3000mm, it supports both structural steel and custom iron profiles. Equipped with advanced CNC controls and hydraulic power systems, the machine achieves high-speed operation with a bending speed of up to 20 mm/sec and a repeatability tolerance within ±0.1 mm. Its robust frame and automated tooling changeover ensure consistent performance in continuous production environments, making it ideal for structural fabrication, heavy plate bending, and custom ironwork.

Durma Sheet Rolling Machine

2500mm x 12mm 0.5 – 5 m/min Made in Turkey

The Durma Sheet Rolling Machine is a precision metal forming tool designed for rolling sheets into cylindrical or conical shapes with smooth, consistent curves. Made in Turkey by Durma, this machine can roll sheets up to 12 mm thickness and maximum width of 2500 mm. It uses heavy-duty steel rollers with diameter options ranging from 300 mm to 600 mm, adjustable for various rolling radii. The machine is powered by a variable frequency drive (VFD) motor, delivering rolling speeds from 0.5 to 5 meters per minute with excellent control. The CNC interface allows programmable rolling profiles for repeatability, while robust mechanical construction ensures long-term reliability and minimal maintenance.

Thoman Mandrel Bending Machine

10 mm – 76 mm Up to 15°/sec Made in Germany

The Thoman Mandrel Bending Machine is a precision industrial solution designed for bending thin-walled tubes and pipes with superior accuracy and minimal deformation. Manufactured in Germany, this machine supports pipe diameters ranging from 10 mm to 76 mm with wall thicknesses up to 4 mm. Equipped with a robust mandrel system, it ensures wrinkle-free bends and maintains tight radius curves, ideal for hydraulic, automotive, and aerospace applications. The machine features a CNC control system enabling programmable bending sequences and angles with repeatability within ±0.2°. With bending speeds of up to 15 degrees per second, the Thoman mandrel bender provides high throughput while preserving material integrity.

Thoman Pipe Bending Machine

20 mm – 130 mm Up to 10°/sec Made in Germany

The Thoman Pipe Bending Machine is a versatile and powerful machine designed for bending a wide range of pipe sizes with high precision. Made in Germany, it can handle pipe diameters from 20 mm up to 130 mm, with maximum wall thicknesses reaching 6 mm. This machine is equipped with hydraulic bending systems and advanced CNC controls that allow for programmable bending angles and multiple bends in a single cycle. It delivers bending speeds up to 10 degrees per second, with a bending radius as tight as 2 times the pipe diameter. The Thoman pipe bender is widely used in industries such as construction, automotive, and shipbuilding due to its reliability and consistent bending quality.

TRUMPF V130 Bending Machine

130 tons 3100 mm Made in Germany

The TRUMPF V130 Bending Machine is a state-of-the-art CNC press brake designed for high-precision bending of sheet metal and plate materials. Manufactured in Germany by TRUMPF, this machine offers a maximum bending force of 130 tons and supports a bending length of up to 3100 mm. Equipped with a highly responsive CNC control system and electric servo drives, the V130 delivers rapid, accurate bending cycles with repeatability within ±0.05 mm. Its versatile tooling options and adaptive backgauges allow complex, multi-axis bending operations, making it ideal for automotive, aerospace, and general metal fabrication industries. The machine’s ergonomic design and advanced safety features ensure operator comfort and productivity.
