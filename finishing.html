Our Surface Finishing Equipment

We utilize state-of-the-art polishing, deburring, and surface treatment equipment to deliver exceptional surface finishes. Our technology and expertise ensure consistent results across various materials and complex geometries, enhancing both aesthetics and functional performance.

Autopullit Flat Polishing Machine

1500 mm width Up to 10 m/min Made in Italy

The Autopullit Flat Polishing Machine is a high-precision surface finishing system designed for achieving superior polish on flat metal surfaces. This Italian-engineered equipment handles workpieces up to 1500mm in width with exceptional consistency. Featuring multiple polishing stations with progressively finer abrasive belts, it produces mirror-like finishes on stainless steel, aluminum, and other metals. The automated feed system operates at speeds up to 10 meters per minute with precise pressure control to ensure uniform results. Advanced dust collection and cooling systems maintain optimal operating conditions, while the programmable interface allows for customized finishing profiles based on material type and desired finish grade. Ideal for architectural panels, decorative elements, and precision industrial components.

garboli pipe polishing machine

2500mm x 12mm 0.5 – 5 m/min Made in Turkey

The Durma Sheet Rolling Machine is a precision metal forming tool designed for rolling sheets into cylindrical or conical shapes with smooth, consistent curves. Made in Turkey by Durma, this machine can roll sheets up to 12 mm thickness and maximum width of 2500 mm. It uses heavy-duty steel rollers with diameter options ranging from 300 mm to 600 mm, adjustable for various rolling radii. The machine is powered by a variable frequency drive (VFD) motor, delivering rolling speeds from 0.5 to 5 meters per minute with excellent control. The CNC interface allows programmable rolling profiles for repeatability, while robust mechanical construction ensures long-term reliability and minimal maintenance.

Timesavers Deburring Machine

1350 mm width Up to 8 m/min Made in Netherlands

The Timesavers Deburring Machine is a high-performance surface finishing system designed for removing burrs, sharp edges, and surface imperfections from metal components. Manufactured in the Netherlands, this industrial-grade machine features a 1350mm working width and can process materials up to 150mm thick. Its multi-stage abrasive belt system progressively refines surfaces while the precision height adjustment ensures consistent material removal across the entire workpiece. Operating at speeds up to 8 meters per minute, the machine delivers high throughput without compromising quality. Advanced dust collection and automated belt tracking systems ensure reliable operation in continuous production environments. Ideal for sheet metal components, laser-cut parts, and precision fabrications requiring smooth, burr-free edges and surfaces.

Our Quality Control Technology

We employ advanced measurement and inspection systems to ensure dimensional accuracy and quality verification for all fabricated components.

InspecVision Reverse Engineering System

0.025mm accuracy Rapid scanning Made in UK

The InspecVision Reverse Engineering System is a state-of-the-art optical measurement solution that provides high-speed, high-accuracy dimensional inspection and reverse engineering capabilities. This UK-manufactured system uses advanced camera technology and specialized lighting to capture detailed component measurements with accuracy up to 0.025mm. The system can scan and digitize parts in seconds, creating precise digital models for comparison against CAD specifications or for reverse engineering applications. Its powerful software suite enables comprehensive measurement reporting, geometric dimensioning and tolerancing (GD&T) analysis, and statistical process control. This technology ensures our quality control processes maintain the highest standards of precision and consistency across all fabricated components.
