Our Welding Technologies

MIG/TIG Welding

Precision MIG/TIG Welding

Our MIG and TIG welding technologies deliver high-strength, clean, and aesthetically refined welds for both structural and precision components. Using advanced welding systems and expert techniques, we ensure minimal distortion and superior weld quality for a wide range of metals and thicknesses.

  • High-strength and durable welds
  • Clean finish with minimal spatter
  • Low heat distortion for delicate parts
  • Ideal for both structural and precision fabrication
Materials: Mild Steel (up to 30mm), Stainless Steel (up to 20mm), Aluminum (up to 15mm), Titanium & Alloys (up to 10mm)

Robotic Welding

Automated Precision Welding

Our robotic welding systems deliver consistent, high-speed, and high-quality welds ideal for large production volumes and complex geometries. With advanced multi-axis robotic arms and programmable precision, we ensure unmatched repeatability, reduced human error, and optimized productivity in both MIG and TIG processes.

  • Consistent weld quality across large batches
  • High-speed automation with multi-axis flexibility
  • Ideal for complex or repetitive weld patterns
  • Reduced labor cost and enhanced safety
Materials: Mild Steel (up to 25mm), Stainless Steel (up to 20mm), Aluminum (up to 12mm), High-Strength Alloys

Automated Corner Welding

Seamless Corner Welding Technology

Our automated corner welding systems are engineered for precise, consistent corner joints with a smooth, flush finish. Ideal for high-end architectural and sanitary applications, this technology automates both welding and grinding processes, ensuring flawless aesthetics and structural integrity in every joint.

  • Uniform, high-quality corner welds
  • Automated grinding for seamless, polished edges
  • Perfect for stainless steel furniture, enclosures, and panels
  • Increases productivity with minimal manual intervention
Materials: Stainless Steel (up to 10mm), Aluminum (up to 8mm), Non-Ferrous Metals

Miller Welding Machine

350 Amps 60% at 350A Made in USA

The Miller XMT 350 CC/CV Multiprocess Welding Machine is a top-tier industrial solution designed for versatility, power, and reliability across a wide range of welding applications. Manufactured in the USA by Miller Electric, this machine delivers a powerful output of up to 350 amps, accommodating processes such as MIG, TIG, Stick, and Flux-Cored welding with ease. With its True Blue® reliability and robust construction, it ensures superior arc performance and minimal downtime in high-demand environments. The XMT 350 features Auto-Line™ Technology, allowing automatic adjustment to various input voltages between 208–575V without manual reconfiguration, making it ideal for dynamic shop conditions.

Robotic Welding Machine

12kg payload MIG, TIG, Spot Welding Made in Japan

The Yaskawa Motoman AR2010 Robotic Welding System is a high-performance automation solution engineered for precision, speed, and consistency in industrial welding environments. Manufactured in Japan, this advanced robotic arm boasts a generous reach of 2010 mm and a payload capacity of up to 12 kg, allowing it to handle complex welding tasks across large workpieces with ease. It supports multiple welding processes including MIG, TIG, and spot welding, ensuring compatibility with various fabrication requirements. Powered by the latest YRC1000 controller, the system offers intuitive programming, seamless integration, and real-time process monitoring. Its repeatability of ±0.02 mm ensures ultra-precise welds, critical for high-tolerance applications such as automotive frames, structural assemblies, and precision metal parts. With built-in collision detection, arc sensing, and fast acceleration speeds, the AR2010 enhances both safety and productivity.

Schnelldorfer Maschinenbau Corner Welding Machine

Internal & External, 90° angles Up to 300 mm Made in Germany

The SCHNELLDORFER CornerWeld 300 is a high-precision automated corner welding solution engineered to deliver clean, consistent welds on 90° sheet metal assemblies. Manufactured in Germany, this machine is purpose-built for fabricators requiring repeatable and visually appealing welds on internal and external corners. Utilizing the TIG welding process, it ensures minimal spatter, perfect arc control, and a refined bead finish on materials like stainless steel, mild steel, and aluminum up to 4 mm thick. With a maximum weld length of 300 mm, the machine handles corner seams with uniform quality and reduced operator intervention. Its Siemens PLC system, coupled with a user-friendly touchscreen HMI, allows for seamless parameter control and real-time diagnostics.
