About

25+ Years of Expertise

Our Founder

50 Years ago Mr. Khalil Ayad started a small company in Riyadh under the name of SIdani Glass & Mirrors co. Through hard work and dedication, Sidani Glass quickly grew into a market leader of the architectural glass processing industry. For this reason, Golden Factory Co. was established in the year 2000 specializing in high-end decorative metal works. Golden Factory Company, also known as Metallia has developed an enviable reputaion for providing quality, reliability, accuracy and attention to detail.

Cutting

State-of-the-art cutting systems for clean, accurate cuts across a wide range of metals and thicknesses—ideal for both structural and detailed fabrication work.

Welding

High-precision MIG, TIG, and robotic welding for strong, clean joints across diverse metals and thicknesses—ensuring structural integrity and visual perfection.

Forming & Shaping

Expert metal forming using advanced bending, rolling, and profiling technologies—delivering accurate shapes and curves for a wide range of industrial applications.

Finishing & Quality Control

Comprehensive surface treatments combined with rigorous inspections to ensure flawless finishes and exacting dimensional standards.
